Frequently Asked Questions

How much does KlipCam Studio cost?

The app is free to download. You buy credits in one-time packs as you need them — no monthly subscription, no auto-renewal. Purchased credits never expire.

Do the photos actually look professional?

Yes. KlipCam Studio uses next-generation AI models trained on professional photography references. Every image is upscaled to 4K. The results are stunning — real couples have used them for wedding invitations, social media, and family gifts.

Which Singapore locations are included?

We cover Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, Jewel Changi, Joo Chiat, Chinatown, Kampong Glam, Botanic Gardens, Fort Canning, MacRitchie Reservoir, the Helix Bridge, CHIJMES, Raffles Hotel, and many more — including historical 1960s Singapore settings.

Absolutely. The Historical Singapore collection lets you recreate vintage Singapore scenes — kopitiams, old National Theatre, Gay World Amusement Park — as a meaningful gift for parents who grew up in that era. It's one of the most emotionally resonant things you can do with KlipCam Studio.

Do I need a professional camera or studio?

No. Just upload a clear photo from your iPhone and choose a collection. KlipCam Studio's AI handles everything else — no equipment, no studio, no professional experience required.

Can I use the photos commercially?

Yes. You retain 100% commercial rights to all photos you generate. Use them for social media, invitations, marketing, or anything else.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your photos are processed securely and never permanently stored on our servers. We never use your images to train AI models.
